    Meet the Russells

    Born and raised in Ontario, Andrew has spent years working on a garlic farm in Southern Ontario before deciding to pursue his dream of running his own farm. This young family now operates Down North Garlic Company out of their property in Owen sound Ontario in hopes that they can provide you with the highest quality garlic with fantastic customer service to match!
